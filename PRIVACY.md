## Privacy Policy (English)

_Last updated: 2026-01-15_

### 1. Overview
**Zbornik** ("the App") is an open-source mobile and web application licensed under the **GNU General Public License v3 (GPLv3)**.

Source code:
https://github.com/m-cho/zbornik

The App is built with **React Native, React Native Web, and Expo**. It does **not require user accounts**, has **no backend services**, and **does not collect personal data**.

### 2. Data Collection
The App does **not collect, process, store, or transmit personal data**.

- No registration or authentication
- No identifiers, emails, or location data
- No data sent to servers controlled by the author

### 3. Local Storage
The App uses **AsyncStorage** exclusively for local functionality.

- Mobile: stored locally on the device
- Web: stored via browser **localStorage**

Stored data includes only non-personal preferences and UI state. Data never leaves the device.

### 4. Analytics
The App currently **does not use analytics**.

If analytics are introduced in the future, they will be minimal, GDPR-compliant, and documented in this policy.

### 5. Third-Party Links
The App contains links to third-party websites, including:
- svetosavlje.org
- External charitable donation websites

The App author is not responsible for third-party privacy practices or content.

### 6. Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

### 7. Changes
This policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date will reflect changes.

### 8. Contact
Privacy-related questions can be submitted via the public repository:
https://github.com/m-cho/zbornik
